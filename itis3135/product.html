Jolly Dolphin Study Club Membership

A simple way to stay consistent with studying. Join the Jolly Dolphin Study Club and get weekly study sprints, friendly support, and a clear plan for web projects.

Join now | See features | Pricing

Features

  • Study Sprints: 25/5 focus sessions to help you finish tasks.
  • Debug Buddy: A simple checklist to fix code without panic.
  • Weekly Theme: We keep projects consistent and easy to follow.
  • Friendly Support: Ask questions and get help with explanations.

How it works

  1. Pick one goal for the week (example: “finish the inventory page”).
  2. Do 2–3 study sprints during the week.
  3. Use the Dolphin Debug checklist when you get stuck.
  4. Share progress and get feedback.
