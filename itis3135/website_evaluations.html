CRAP Website Evaluations

Apple

  • Contrast: Excellent contrast between text and background, making content easy to read.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, colors, and layout across all pages.
  • Alignment: Clean alignment that guides the eye naturally through the page.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped clearly, improving organization and usability.

LingsCars

  • Contrast: Overuse of bright colors causes visual overload and confusion.
  • Repetition: Little consistency in fonts, images, and layout.
  • Alignment: Elements feel scattered with no clear visual flow.
  • Proximity: Unrelated content is placed too close together, making it hard to focus.
